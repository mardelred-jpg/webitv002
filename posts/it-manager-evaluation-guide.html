What a manager should really evaluate before hiring an external IT service

2026-03-08 4 min read

Before deciding, an SME manager should not only ask who resolves incidents better, but which model best accompanies the current stage of the company. The truly important variables are total cost, the ability to grow or reduce structure, access to specialisation and the operational risk derived from depending on few people.

Finding the right fit

If the company seeks financial predictability, flexibility and broad coverage, the external service usually offers a clear advantage. If it needs constant control, significant physical presence or a very close integration with internal processes, an in-house or hybrid team may fit better.

The best decision is usually not the most ideological one, but the one most aligned with the stage of the business. In an SME, the goal is not to have "more IT", but to have the right capacity to sustain operations, protect growth and avoid unnecessary rigidity.
