Alternative measures to the LGD quota: when can they be used?

2025-03-05 4 min read

For some companies, especially those with highly specialized hiring needs, direct compliance with the 2% quota may not be simple. Spanish law allows alternative measures, but only in exceptional cases and with prior justification.

Are alternative measures automatic?

No. Alternative measures are not a free choice. The company must first obtain a declaration of exceptionality, usually because it could not fill the position through the employment system or because there are proven productive, organizational, technical or economic reasons that make direct incorporation especially difficult.

What counts as an alternative measure?

The law allows several substitute options. These include contracting goods or services from a Special Employment Center, working with a self-employed person with a disability, or making monetary donations to foundations or associations whose purpose includes labor inclusion and job creation for people with disabilities.

Why documentation matters

This area is highly document-dependent. If the company cannot prove the reason for exceptionality and the validity of the measure used, it risks appearing compliant in practice while remaining weak from a legal audit perspective.
