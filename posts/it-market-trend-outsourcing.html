The trend is clear: the market is moving towards outsourced IT services

2026-03-08 4 min read

Market evolution clearly points towards outsourced and managed service models, especially as technical complexity, cybersecurity demands and the difficulty of hiring specialised talent grow. In this context, many companies are no longer seeing IT outsourcing as a tactical solution and are starting to use it as a structural part of their operations.

The numbers speak

Market data reinforces this reading. An industry report places the global managed services market at $430.56 billion in 2026 and projects $704.2 billion by 2031, with a compound annual growth rate of 10.34%.

This growth does not mean that in-house IT will disappear. What it does indicate is that the market increasingly rewards models capable of offering specialisation, flexibility and scaling without turning the entire technological effort into fixed structure.

For an SME, this trend has a practical takeaway: outsourcing is no longer a "provisional" solution, but a mature and increasingly normalised option.
