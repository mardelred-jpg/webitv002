In-house IT department: when it still makes sense for an SME

2026-03-08 3 min read

Not all companies should outsource all their IT. An in-house team continues to add value when the organisation needs daily control, strong alignment with internal processes or constant presence over operations and users.

When internal knowledge weighs more

It can also be the best option when the business depends on highly customised systems, technology decisions closely tied to the company's core or a particularly sensitive regulatory environment. In those cases, internal knowledge and proximity to the business weigh more than the elasticity of the external model.

However, even in these situations, many companies do not forgo specialised external support. Common practice is to keep governance and critical knowledge in-house, and rely externally on support, security, monitoring or specific projects.
